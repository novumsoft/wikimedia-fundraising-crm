How to review dedupe items

Usually, all items in a work batch have been machine-recommended for the same resolution. The main goal of reviewing these items is to find instances where the machine may have made a mistake. Click these rows once to exclude from the automatic resolution. These rows will be dimmed, and when the spinner completes, your choice has been successfully stored on the server.
